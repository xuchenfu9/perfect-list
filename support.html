Frequently Asked Questions

General

Q: What platforms does Simple Tasks support?

A: iPhone, iPad, Mac (menu bar app), and Apple Watch. Home screen and lock screen widgets are supported on iOS.

Q: Is Simple Tasks free?

A: Yes. Basic features are free (up to 2 categories, 25 tasks, and 3 default habits). Upgrade to Premium for unlimited access.

Tasks

Q: How do I create a task?

A: Tap the "+" button next to a category name.

Q: How do I delete a task?

A: Swipe left on a task. Deleted tasks go to Trash, where you can restore or permanently delete them.

Q: How do I reorder categories?

A: Tap the sort icon to enter reorder mode, then drag to rearrange. You can also long-press a category title for reorder options.

Q: Can I attach a location to a task?

A: Yes. In the task detail view, tap "Set Location" to search and attach a location. You can then open it in your preferred maps app for navigation.

Q: Can I attach photos or voice notes?

A: Yes. In the task detail view, you can add photos from your library or camera, and record voice notes with automatic transcription.

Sync & Backup

Q: How does iCloud sync work?

A: If you are signed into iCloud, your tasks and habits automatically sync across all your Apple devices. No additional setup required.

Q: My tasks are not syncing. What should I do?

A:

  1. Ensure all devices are signed into the same Apple ID.
  2. Ensure iCloud is enabled for Simple Tasks: Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Simple Tasks.
  3. Ensure your device has an active internet connection.
  4. Sync may be delayed under poor network conditions — wait a few minutes.

Q: How do I back up my data?

A: Go to Settings > Data Backup > Export. Tasks are exported as a JSON file you can save anywhere.

Q: How do I restore from a backup?

A: Go to Settings > Data Backup > Restore, then select a .mylistbackup file. You can also receive backups via AirDrop.

Calendar

Q: How does calendar sync work?

A: When you set a due date, the task is automatically added to your system calendar. Completing or deleting the task removes the event.

Q: Is calendar permission required?

A: No. Calendar access is optional — only needed if you want due-date tasks to appear in your system calendar.

Premium & Subscriptions

Q: What does Premium include?

A: Unlimited categories, unlimited tasks, and custom habit creation.

Q: What plans are available?

A: Monthly, Yearly, and Lifetime (one-time purchase).

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?

A: Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device.

Q: How do I restore a previous purchase?

A: In the app, go to Settings > Premium and tap "Restore Purchases".

Widgets

Q: How do I add a widget?

A: Long-press your home screen, tap "+" in the top-left corner, search for "Simple Tasks", and choose a size.

Q: My widget is not updating.

A: Widgets update based on iOS system scheduling. Try removing and re-adding the widget.

System Requirements

  • iOS 16.0 or later
  • macOS 13.0 or later
  • watchOS (companion app)

常见问题

基本使用

问：任务清单支持哪些平台？

答：iPhone、iPad、Mac（菜单栏应用）和 Apple Watch。iOS 支持主屏幕和锁屏小组件。

问：任务清单是免费的吗？

答：是的，基础功能免费（最多 2 个分类、25 个任务和 3 个默认习惯）。升级会员可解锁无限使用。

任务管理

问：如何创建任务？

答：点击分类名称旁的"+"按钮。

问：如何删除任务？

答：在任务上向左滑动。删除的任务进入回收站，可恢复或永久删除。

问：如何调整分类顺序？

答：点击排序图标进入排序模式，拖动调整顺序。也可长按分类标题弹出排序选项。

问：可以为任务设置位置吗？

答：可以。在任务详情页点击"设置位置"搜索并添加位置，之后可用地图应用打开导航。

问：可以添加照片或语音备忘录吗？

答：可以。在任务详情页，您可以从相册或相机添加照片，也可以录制语音备忘录并自动转文字。

同步与备份

问：iCloud 同步如何工作？

答：登录 iCloud 后，任务和习惯自动在所有 Apple 设备间同步，无需额外设置。

问：任务没有同步怎么办？

答：

  1. 确保所有设备登录同一 Apple ID。
  2. 确保 iCloud 已启用：设置 > Apple ID > iCloud > 任务清单。
  3. 确保设备已联网。
  4. 网络不佳时同步可能有延迟，请稍等几分钟。

问：如何备份数据？

答：进入 设置 > 数据备份 > 导出备份，任务将导出为 JSON 文件。

问：如何恢复备份？

答：进入 设置 > 数据备份 > 恢复备份，选择 .mylistbackup 文件。也可通过隔空投送接收。

日历同步

问：日历同步如何工作？

答：设置截止日期后，任务自动添加到系统日历。完成或删除任务时，日历事件自动移除。

问：必须授权日历权限吗？

答：不需要。日历权限是可选的，仅在您希望任务出现在系统日历中时才需要。

会员与订阅

问：会员包含什么？

答：无限分类、无限任务和自定义打卡习惯。

问：有哪些订阅方案？

答：月付、年付和终身（一次性购买）。

问：如何取消订阅？

答：在设备上进入 设置 > Apple ID > 订阅。

问：如何恢复购买？

答：在应用内进入 设置 > 会员，点击"恢复购买"。

小组件

问：如何添加小组件？

答：长按主屏幕空白处，点击左上角"+"，搜索"任务清单"，选择尺寸。

问：小组件不更新怎么办？

答：小组件由 iOS 系统调度更新。尝试移除后重新添加。

系统要求

  • macOS 13.0 或更高版本
